Features

Users

You can log in as one of three different roles: administrator, teacher and students. As administrator you have acces to all the features including: calendar, list of students and teachers, creating accounts, statistics and many editing options.

Students

Calendar

Calendar allows to schedule all the lessons and important events. When logged in as administrator you can filter lessons by teacher, student or course, edit them, drag and resize. When logged as teacher or student you have acces only your lessons without editing options.

Calendar

Statistics

As administrator you have access to different statistics about number of lessons, students, percentage of attending in each course and many more.
